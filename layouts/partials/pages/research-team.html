About UXPH Research

The objective of the UXPH Research team is to leverage existing industry-academe connection in gaining better understanding of the UX landscape in the PH Context. This can be a potential income-generating activity and a training ground for those who want to engage in research.

Specifically, UXPH Research team shall:

  • contribute to the body of knowledge towards the better understanding of the UX and HCI landscape in the Philippines
  • work its partners in the collection of data, design of experiments that are ethical and scientifically-sound
  • share and make its findings accessible to the PH context towards a more data-driven design-mature UX Strategy landscape
  • provide an avenue for young and budding researchers to take part in research activities
  • actively find, secure and procure funding, equipment and other resources that helps in the research process

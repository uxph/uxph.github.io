Frequently Asked Questions

Do participants need to bring anything for workshops?

Always bring a pen or pencil and paper to take notes. Specific requirements for each workshop will be announced to email attendees and on the website. Internet will be provided in the venue.

Are tickets refundable or transferable?

Tickets are non-refundable, but are transferable. Please send us an email to info@uxph.org and cc the details of the attending participants.

Will lunch be served during the event?

No, lunch is not provided as part of the event. Our tickets are affordably priced to provide access to speakers, workshops, basic workshop materials, and some awesome UXPH swag. You may bring food and drinks inside the venue. Just remember to practice Clean As You Go (CLAYGO)!

How can I get a ticket? How much are they?

Tickets are available online via Eventbrite using PayPal. Prices vary according per event and are announced online. A voucher will be sent to your email - please present a printed or digital copy on the event day.

Do you have student pricing?

Currently, we do not have student pricing, as our tickets are already reasonably priced to accommodate students.

Are there walk-in tickets?

For Cebu, we cannot accept any more walk-ins for the event since all tickets are sold out already. You may get a ticket for Davao or Manila instead!

Are you looking for sponsors? Will you have booths at the event?

Yes! If your company would like to be a partner or purchase a booth to promote your company, please email us at info@uxph.org.

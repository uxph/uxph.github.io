About UXPH Research

We are a group of researchers who wish to understand the UX landscape in the Philippine context. We envision ourselves to be the foremost source of reports and trends that describe how UX is immersed in various industries in the country. Our mission is to empower Filipinos to use data-driven approaches in designing solutions to address problems and pain points in our nation.

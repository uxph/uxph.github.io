UXPH National Roadshow: Cebu

August 31, 2019
8:00am - 5:00pm
The Company CEBU, IT Park




Description

On the 31st of August, we will be celebrating the Cebu leg of the UXPH National Roadshow themed "Cultivating Design Culture." This is part of a national effort to spread the knowledge of user experience design not only to tech professionals but also to business who would greatly benefit from its processes and methodology.

For this event, we are expecting over 100 professionals not only in visual design, industrial design, user interface design and user experience design, but also professionals who are interested in product development with applications in both tech and non-tech businesses.
